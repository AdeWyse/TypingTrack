About

Typing Track is an app to show how fast you can type. Be it dummy texts, random words, or even fully-fledged texts. It's ideal to see how many words you can type per minute (wpm), practice touch typing, or just have some fun.

While you are typing it shows instant feedback on whether you typed right or wrong and also on which word you are using easy to see colors. Don't worry if you got something wrong, that's normal, you can always backtrack. In the end of each run, all the words correctly typed are counted and it shows you your results With each new run more data is available to make the results even more accurate. This is important since our typing seed can vary greatly between days and even hours on the same day.
